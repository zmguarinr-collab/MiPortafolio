Migration (2023)

Synopsis: An animated journey about overcoming fears, embracing family bonds, and exploring the world beyond our comfort zone.

ENGLISH:

Migration is an animated adventure about the Mallard family: Mack, Pam, their kids Dax and Gwen, and Uncle Dan. They live a safe but boring life in a New England pond. While Mack wants to stay forever, Pam convinces him to open his eyes and migrate to Jamaica.

During their journey, they get lost and ended up heading north. They faced a big storm and took shelter with a scary but kind heron named Erin. Later, they arrive in New York City, where they meet a tough pigeon named Chump and free Delroy, a caged Jamaican macaw. Delroy gratefully agrees to guide them to paradise.

However, an evil human chef is chasing them because they ruined his restaurant. The family helps a group of farm ducks escape, but Dax loses his wing feathers and can't fly. Soon, the chef captures the adult birds using a helicopter, but the siblings manage to hide.

In the end, Dax and Gwen save their parents with the help of Uncle Dan and Delroy. The Mallards finally reach Jamaica and enjoy the warm weather. Next spring, they will start a new adventure helping some lost penguins get to the South Pole.

ESPAÑOL:

Office Romance (2026)

Synopsis: A classic romantic comedy filled with workplace tension, secret relationships, and the fun clichés of the "enemies-to-lovers" genre.

ENGLISH:

Office Romance is a romantic comedy starring Jennifer Lopez as Jackie, a strict airline CEO, and Brett Goldstein as Daniel, a charming new lawyer. Jackie loves her job and enforces a severe rule: workplace relationships are strictly forbidden. She believes romance only creates chaos in the office.

However, everything changes when the company faces a major lawsuit. Jackie is forced to work closely with Daniel to solve the problem. While they are preparing the case, their professional tension quickly turns into an intense attraction. They started as rivals, but they soon fell in love.

The situation gets complicated during a business trip to the Dominican Republic, where they have to hide their secret affair from the rest of the team. In the end, Jackie will have to choose between her strict rules or following her heart.

Synopsis: An intense Spanish psychological thriller about identity, memory loss, and a dangerous police investigation full of dark secrets.

ENGLISH:

La desconocida is a psychological thriller directed by Gabe Ibáñez. The story begins in the port of Barcelona, where the police find a mysterious woman tied up inside a cargo container. At the hospital, doctors discover that she suffers from severe amnesia; she does not remember her name or her past.

The case becomes a dangerous race against time when an assassin tries to kill her in her medical bed. Detective Anna Ripoll, who is dealing with her own past traumas, takes the case alongside her partner Quique. While they are investigating the docks, they realize that someone wants to silence the woman at all costs.

They discovered a few clues, but the truth is hidden behind a web of criminal secrets. Soon, the detectives will discover that "the unknown woman" is the key to exposing a massive conspiracy.

San Andreas (2015)

Synopsis: A thrilling action movie about an aerial rescue pilot who faces the biggest earthquake in history to save his family.

ENGLISH:

San Andreas is a disaster film starring Dwayne Johnson as Ray Gaines, a brave rescue pilot. The story starts when a massive earthquake destroys the Hoover Dam. Soon, seismologist Lawrence Hayes discovers that the San Andreas fault is activating, which triggers a catastrophic 9.6 magnitude earthquake along California.

Amid the chaos, Ray rescues his ex-wife Emma in Los Angeles. Together, they travel to San Francisco to save their daughter, Blake, who is trapped in a collapsing building. Blake survives thanks to Ben and Ollie, two British brothers. While the family is trying to reunite, a giant tsunami hits the city, flooding the streets and sinking buildings.

Ray navigated through the water and saved Blake just before she drowned. He performed CPR and successfully revived her. In the end, the family reunites at a refugee camp. They will rebuild their lives together while looking at a completely altered California landscape.

The Curious Case of Benjamin Button (2008)

Synopsis: A deeply emotional and poetic masterpiece about a man who is born old and ages backward, exploring love, time, and human destiny.

ENGLISH:

The Curious Case of Benjamin Button is a drama film directed by David Fincher. The story begins in a New Orleans hospital, where an elderly woman named Daisy is dying as Hurricane Katrina approaches. She asks her daughter, Caroline, to read aloud the diary of Benjamin Button.

The diary reveals that Benjamin was born with the physical appearance of an 81-year-old man. Abandoned by his father, a kind woman named Queenie raised him in a nursing home. There, he met Daisy when they were children. As time passes, Benjamin is growing younger while everyone else gets older.

Grammar Cards: Mastering Verb Tenses

Present Simple

Forming Facts & Routines

I walk to school / He walks to school.

💡 ¿Cuándo se usa?: Para verdades generales, hechos permanentes, estados actuales y rutinas diarias.

🔧 ¿Cómo se forma?:
• Con I, You, We, They ➔ El verbo se queda igual.
• Con He, She, It ➔ ¡Regla de oro! Agrega -s o -es al final del verbo.

❌ Negativo: Usa Don't o Doesn't + verbo en forma base.

Present Continuous

Forming Actions in Progress

They are swimming / She is running.

💡 ¿Cuándo se usa?: Para cosas que están pasando **justo ahora**, en este preciso momento, o situaciones muy temporales.

🔧 ¿Cómo se forma?:
Fórmula obligatoria: Verb To Be (Am / Is / Are) + Verbo terminado en -ING.

⚠️ Secreto: Si el verbo termina en consonante-vocal-consonante (como run o swim), duplica la última letra antes del -ing (running / swimming).

Past Simple (Regular)

Forming Completed Actions

We watched the movie / They clapped.

💡 ¿Cuándo se usa?: Para acciones que comenzaron y **terminaron por completo** en el pasado.

🔧 ¿Cómo se forma?:
Toma el verbo en su forma base y agrégale la terminación -ed al final. Funciona igual para todas las personas (I, He, They, etc.).

❌ Negativo: Para decir "no", usa **Didn't** y el verbo vuelve a su forma normal (Ej: I didn't watch).

Past Simple (Irregular)

Forming Changing Verbs

I went home (Go → Went) / She ate (Eat → Ate).

💡 ¿Cuándo se usa?: Igual que el anterior (acciones terminadas), pero con verbos rebeldes que no aceptan la "-ed".

🔧 ¿Cómo se forma?:
No hay fórmula fija; el verbo cambia su escritura por completo.

💡 Alivio gramatical: En **negativo**, el truco es el mismo: Usa **Didn't** y el verbo regresa a la normalidad (Ej: I didn't go). ¡Ya no necesitas el irregular!

Future (Will)

Forming Instant Decisions & Predictions

It will rain tomorrow / I will help you.

💡 ¿Cuándo se usa?: Para predicciones del futuro, promesas o decisiones rápidas que tomas en el mismo instante en que hablas.

🔧 ¿Cómo se forma?:
Es el tiempo más fácil: Coloca la palabra auxiliar WILL + el verbo completamente normal. No cambia con ninguna persona.

❌ Negativo: Puedes usar Will not o su contracción nativa: Won't (Ej: I won't go).

Future (Going to)

Forming Intentions & Plans

I am going to study / They are going to travel.

💡 ¿Cuándo se usa?: Para planes futuros confirmados, intenciones claras o cuando tienes evidencias lógicas de que algo va a pasar.

🔧 ¿Cómo se forma?:
Usa el **Verb To Be (Am / Is / Are) + GOING TO + el verbo normal**.

✏️ Ejemplo de evidencia: Look at those black clouds! It is going to rain. (¡Mira esas nubes negras! Va a llover).
